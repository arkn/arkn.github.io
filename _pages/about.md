Ken Arai is a scientific researcher at Sony R&D, where he works at the intersection of artificial intelligence and human cognition. His background spans both industry and academia, with 13 years at GE HealthCare, where he developed FDA-cleared AI applications for medical imaging (MRI, X-ray CT), and research at the University of Tokyo, where he explored human-computer interaction and embodied cognition. This dual perspective enables him to translate theoretical insights into practical solutions that have a tangible impact.
